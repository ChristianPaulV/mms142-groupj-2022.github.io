The ethical ramifications of AI-generated images are concerning and their potential to change society, its laws, and bylaws around the internet, and Netiquette is huge. Jiang (2022) presented an article wherein an art thief “captured” an ongoing Livestream of an artist’s painting, then proceeded to use an AI Image Generator to finish the work before the artist did so.

A repeat of a similar situation could be an influential factor for artists to eventually avoid posting WIPs (work-in-progress art) or streaming their work in the future. Thankfully, the thief did not get away with it, but it has still set a precedent in the art world.
