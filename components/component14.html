2. Low-cost Learning
For some people, pursuing art is a life calling. They cannot simply detach themselves from it for the sake of practicality.

Treading this creative path can exhaust one’s resources because opportunities and materials that can help improve and nurture artistic skills tend to be expensive. And this is where AI can be truly helpful in furthering aspiring artists’ learning competencies. One only needs to have access to the internet and a decent understanding of how the new technology works to explore different art styles and mediums that they most resonate with.

Furthermore, there are a handful of free open-source AI Image Generators that they can try out so they will be able to experiment and discover new knowledge without notable hindering constraints on their part.
