Understand how AI Image Generators are changing Creative Industries

AI Image Generators

The quality of an AI Image Generator’s output would depend on the caliber of the AI tool and the user’s text prompts. It is therefore important to choose the correct AI Image Generator for the best results.  However, there are hundreds of AI Image Generation services present on the web, and some are paid for while others are not.

In general, it is quite intuitive to use these AI Image Generators as the user would only need to write in a text field the prompt for the image they wish to create. The AI tool would then generate an image or a set of images that would best match the user’s prompt.

When it comes to image generation, AI Image Generators can compose them in a number of ways. In Midjourney for example, after selecting from the generated images, the user can choose to upscale the resolution and/or generate more “varied” altered versions of a previously generated image. The user can also run through an image recursively until they are satisfied with the result.

Midjourney generating an image using a command prompt.
Essentially, if one is creative enough with their prompts, a promising AI Image Generator can deliver. This means that “weak” prompts that are inputted into AI Image Generators may produce not-so-great results.

There are also cases wherein it is not the fault of the user as there are visual errors within the image due to gaps in the AI tool’s training. For example, a generated image containing a human figure may have wrong proportions such as having multiple arms or anatomically incorrect hands. As a result, professional artists or enthusiasts edit these errors on their own image-editing software. In this way, people can create “art” without the need to follow the usual creative process.

As an alternative use, however, artists may also utilize AI Image Generators to augment their creative process, this is mostly done by generating images...

1... to help them roughly visualize a planned artwork.
2. aid them in the generation of ideas for concept art/design.
3. or generate various images so that they may use them for photo bashing. Photo bashing is an act of cutting out an element from an image (Stock Photo, 3D renders, etc.) and then incorporating it within a larger artwork with heavy edits so that it fits visually. (Duff, 2022; see also Blevins, 2022).

Accessibility Review

In the list of AI Image Generators provided above, accessibility can be reviewed in terms of costs, hardware requirement, or third-party integration.

ON COST:

•  From the given table, it is clear that not all AI Image Generators come for free.
• Some offer a limited trial period before asking the user to pay for their services. Midjourney, for example, has a limit of 25 artworks for their free trial. Users exceeding the limit are required to subscribe to a plan. DALL-E, on the other hand, uses a “credit” system, users spend “credits” to generate a set of images. Free users start with 50 credits and receive an additional 15 credits every month.
• For the common folk, paid subscriptions may be judged as too expensive, especially if the said user would only use AI Image Generation as a novelty.
• For professional artists/enthusiasts that are interested in incorporating AI tools in their workflow or subsequent projects, an investment in a proper AI art generator could prove to be very helpful.

ON HARDWARE REQUIREMENT:

• Although most of the AI Image generators provided are accessible to the public, some like Stable Diffusion requires mid-level to high-end hardware to operate smoothly. This is because Stable Diffusion is more powerful in comparison to other image generators like DeepAI .
• There are AI Image Generators that are specifically designed for smart mobile devices, like StarryAI.

ON THIRD PARTY INTEGRATION:

• Some AI tools require users to have another third-party account, Midjourney, for example, requires Discord (a instant messaging social platform) for interaction with its features.
• Some AI tools also require account creation or having their program installed.

Besides being a powerful image generator (Stable Diffusion 2, Midjourney, and Dall-E 2), accessibility is an important factor in the disruptiveness of a piece of technology. If the tool is highly accessible, it can spread much faster to other user bases. For example, Starry AI is a free mobile application that can target smartphone users that caught wind of the AI Text-to-Image technology.
From the information listed above, it can be highly suggested that the most accessible AI Image Generators from the list are those that are completely free and are based on cloud services (Craiyon and Latent Diffusion). This is because they are accessible on the go and present on various platforms/devices. It is to be noted, however, that accessibility does not translate to quality performance and one should also look into the AI tool’s individual capability as well.

General Disruption

The breakthrough technology of AI Image Generation pioneers the exploration of new artistic frameworks in creative industries. Just recently an AI image generated from DALL-E 2 made its mark as the First AI-designed magazine cover (Liu, 2022).
Image Description: The World’s Smartest Artificial Intelligence Just Made Its First Magazine Cover
Several companies have also been starting to utilize this technology for their design visualizations and marketing. With the launch of its Air Max Scorpion, Nike unlocked the future of AI-designed footwear. Designers at Nike also highlight that collaborating with AI Image Generators substantially moves forward the designing workflow by delivering efficiency, and productivity, and giving a different feel to artistic control (Taylor, 2022).
Image Description: Artificial Intelligence Designed These Dainty Nike Sneakers
For the general public, generated AI tools are gaining more traction. For example, Canva, a free online design platform commonly used by more than 100 million individuals and businesses, recently launched a text-to-image feature (powered by Stable Diffusion). The availability of these AI features promises to “empower users to create unique images and art from a simple description in seconds” (Text to Image in Canva, 2022). Consequently, these application gives the populace a whole new ability to generate and produce art with massive comfort on their end to meet their personal or commercial needs.

Positive Disruption

1. AI Improves/Changes Artists’ workflow
Artificial Intelligence helps lessen the time needed to produce work and is especially useful when an artist is bound by a time crunch (AI Art Shop, 2022). With AI Image Generators, it only takes a few parameters to have an artificially-engineered piece whose components, symmetry, and accuracy are dependent on how detailed the prompt is. The breakthrough of this technology and its introduction to the public offers a new look and new approach for the creative industry to try out. In a November 2022 article by California State University, a Bachelor of Fine Arts student shared how they were able to come up with a painting using a series of AI-generated artworks for brainstorming and as references.
2. Low-cost Learning
For some people, pursuing art is a life calling. They cannot simply detach themselves from it for the sake of practicality. Treading this creative path can exhaust one’s resources because opportunities and materials that can help improve and nurture artistic skills tend to be expensive. And this is where AI can be truly helpful in furthering aspiring artists’ learning competencies. One only needs to have access to the internet and a decent understanding of how the new technology works to explore different art styles and mediums that they most resonate with. Furthermore, there are a handful of free open-source AI Image Generators that they can try out so they will be able to experiment and discover new knowledge without notable hindering constraints on their part.

3. With AI, the Aesthetics of Art and People’s Knowledge of Art History Will Expand
The development of AI Image generators opens a lot of possibilities, especially for the creative industry. While there is still no consensus reached regarding its ethical violations accusations, and whether the outcome once its full potential is unleashed will, end up being more destructive than helpful, it is undeniable that AI generator tools are more than capable of birthing a new art movement and expanding the aesthetics of art for the better as it offers unique takes and new insights for the creatives to learn from.

Additionally, users that want to get proficient at writing prompts are also inevitably exposed to much deeper art history as they determine which art styles should complement their AI-generated image.

Negative Disruption

— AI Threatens the Livelihood of Digital Artists
Digital artists had always been held in lower regard compared to traditional artists. This is due to some people associating digital art with cheat codes and a handful of art enhancement tools readily available online.

Unfortunately, with the introduction of AI image Generators, the prejudice that digital artwork can be produced with just a few clicks is further reinforced. This time, for real. Its implications include more competition for artists when it comes to art commissions, and even threatening some art professions to become obsolete. While a study by Gu and Li (2020) shows that art experts prefer traditionally made paintings by living artists over AI-generated ones, and are likely to reject the latter once they learned of its “creator’s” identity, it was also determined that non-experts did not have a significant change in their liking rating and purchase intent—showing indifference to the fact the artworks are artificially-procured.
